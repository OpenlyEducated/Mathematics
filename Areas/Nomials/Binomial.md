
# Binomial

$Binomial = Monomial + Monomial$ <br>
$= x_{0}^{x_{0}} * \cdots + x_{0}^{x_{0}} * \cdots$ <br>
$x \neq 0$ <br>
$n \geq 0$ <br>

<br>
<br>

## Examples

- $x + y = x^{1} + y^{1}$

- $3x^{2} - xy^{4} = 3^{1} * x^{2} + (-1)^{1} * x^{1} * y^{4}$

<br>
<br>

## Expansions

-   $a^{2} - b^{2} = (a - b)(a + b)$
