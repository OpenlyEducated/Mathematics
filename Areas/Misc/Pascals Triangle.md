## Cell Calculation

$\huge \left( \begin{array}{c} row \\\\ column \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} n \\\\ k \end{array} \right) = \frac{n!}{k! (n - k)!}$

<br>

$\huge \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\\\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \frac{4!}{2! (4 - 2)!} = \frac{24}{2 * 2} = 6$
