
# Exponentiation

<br>

$\large         Base^{ Exponent}$ <br>
$\large = Base^{ Power}$ <br>
$\large = b^{ n}$

<br>
<br>

## Positive Exponent

<br>

$\huge b^{ n} = b * b * \cdots$

$\huge b^{ 2} = b * b$

<br>

$\huge 2^{ 3} = 2 * 2 * 2$

<br>
<br>

## Negative Exponent

<br>

$\huge b^{ - n} = \frac{ 1 }{ b^{ n} }$

$\huge b^{ - 2} = \frac{ 1 }{ b * b }$

<br>

$\huge 2^{ - 3} = \frac{ 1 }{ 2 * 2 * 2 }$

<br>
<br>

## Tower Of Power

$\huge x^{ a^{ b}}$

$\huge 4^{ 3^{ 2}} = 4^{ 9}$

<br>
<br>

## Operations

<br>

$\huge (x^{ a})^{ b} = x^{ a * b}$

<br>

$\huge a^{ n} * b^{ n} = ( a * b )^{ n}$

<br>

$\huge x^{ a}  *   x^{ b} = x^{ a + b}$

$\huge x^{ a} \div x^{ b} = x^{ a - b}$

<br>
