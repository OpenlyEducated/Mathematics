
# Exponentiation

<br>

$\large         Base^{ Exponent}$ <br>
$\large = Base^{ Power}$ <br>
$\large = b^{ n}$

<br>
<br>

## Positive Exponent

<br>

$\huge b^{ n} = b * b * \cdots$

$\huge b^{ 1} = b$

$\huge b^{ 0} = 1$

<br>

### Examples

$\huge b^{ 3} = b * b * b$

$\huge 2^{ 3} = 2 * 2 * 2$

<br>
<br>

## Negative Exponent

<br>

$\huge b^{ - n} = \frac{ 1 }{ b^{ n} }$

$\huge b^{ - 1} = \frac{ 1 }{ b }$

<br>

### Examples

$\huge 2^{ - 3} = \frac{ 1 }{ 2 * 2 * 2 }$

<br>
<br>

## Operations

<br>

$\huge x^{ a^{ b}} = x^{ a * b}$

<br>

$\huge x^{ a}  *   x^{ b} = x^{ a + b}$

$\huge x^{ a} \div x^{ b} = x^{ a - b}$
