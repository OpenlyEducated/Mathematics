
# Exponentiation

<br>

$\large         Base^{ Exponent}$ <br>
$\large = Base^{ Power}$ <br>
$\large = b^{ n}$

<br>
<br>

## Positive Exponent

$\huge b^{ n} = b * b * \cdots$

$\huge b^{ 3} = b * b * b$

$\huge 2^{ 3} = 2 * 2 * 2$

<br>
<br>

## Negative Exponent

$\huge b^{ - n} = \frac{ 1 }{ b^{ n} }$

$\huge 2^{ - 3} = \frac{ 1 }{ 2 * 2 * 2 }$
